Class: Table

Attributes:
length (float)
color (string)
has_tablecloth (boolean)
seating_capacity (integer)

Methods:

add_chair (adds 1 to the seating_capacity attribute)
paint_table (changes the color attribute)
remove_tablecloth (changes the has_tablecloth attribute to false)
open_leaves (adds 20.0 to the length attribute)
