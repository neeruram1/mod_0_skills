Class: Oven

Attributes:
is_on (boolean)
temperature (integer)
cook_time (float)
brand (string)
is_closed (boolean)

Methods:

turn_on (changes the is_on attribute to true)
raise_temperature (adds 10 to the temperature attribute)
add_cook_time (adds 5.0 to the cook_time attribute)
open_oven (changes the is_closed attribute to false)
replace_oven (changes the brand attribute)
